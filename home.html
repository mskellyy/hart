HART Salon is a full-service beauty destination designed for those who seek quality without compromise, meaningful rituals, and care that feels personal. We specialize in creating elevated beauty experiences from bespoke head spa treatments rooted in Japanese wellness to precision nail, and lash services. HART offers a sanctuary of refinement and calm, where every detail is thoughtfully curated to help you look and feel your best. Whether you're stepping in for a moment of restoration or a full transformation, our goal is the same: for you to leave feeling confident, refreshed, and deeply cared for.

We see beauty as a form of empowerment a ritual that reconnects you to your confidence. We believe every client deserves to feel radiant, and through thoughtful services, and personalized care, we help transform routine appointments into meaningful experiences. This isn't just about looking good it's about feeling aligned, and restored
